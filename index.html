Slides-O-Matic Sync up a slide deck to video or audio

Alpha Testing Currently optimized for Chrome. Other browsers, including mobile, in progess.

Because your presentation is more
than just your slides

Create A Presentation

Present In Style

You've spent a lot of effort on your presentation. You even recorded it.

Don't just post your slides. Share them the way they were originally intended.

Sync up your recording with your slides.

Completely Free

No user account or login is required.

Use a video or audio file and slide deck that you've already uploaded to another site.

After you time your slides to your media, you get a link that you can use to view your presentation or edit it later.

Enhance Your Presentation

Set when your video will go to full screen mode.

Include a website as a slide so the viewer can interact with it during the presentation.
(Only some websites allow this.)

Add a pause during the presentation so that viewers know when to explore.

Media Sources

For slides, you can use Speaker Deck , SlideShare , Google Drive , or Slid.es.
(Limited support for Slid.es.)

For videos, you can use YouTube or Vimeo.

Want to add sound instead of video? Use SoundCloud.
