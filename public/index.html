Present in Style

You've spent a lot of effort on your presentation. You even recorded it. Don't just post your slides. Share them the way you intended.

Fast & Easy

No user account or login is required. Use a video or audio file and slide deck that you've already uploaded to another site. After you time your slides to your media, you get a link that you can use to view your presentation or edit it later.

Media

For slides, you can use Speaker Deck , SlideShare , or Google Slides

For videos, you can use YouTube or Vimeo.

Want to add sound instead of video? Use SoundCloud.
